# Privacy Policy & Support – Nutraxis

**Last updated:** January 2026

Nutraxis is a health and wellness application designed to provide personalized nutrition insights and habit-tracking features to support healthier daily choices.

---

## Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Nutraxis may collect limited personal data such as user identifiers and usage information in order to:

- Provide core app functionality  
- Personalize wellness insights  
- Improve app performance and user experience  

Nutraxis does **not collect sensitive medical data** and does **not provide medical diagnoses or treatment**.

All data is handled securely and in accordance with Apple App Store policies.  
We do **not sell personal data to third parties**.

Subscriptions and purchases are managed securely through **Apple In-App Purchases**.

---

## Data We May Collect

To provide the features of the application, Nutraxis may collect limited information such as:

- Account information (for example email address if you create an account)
- Nutrition and wellness information you voluntarily enter (meals, water intake, activity data)
- Usage analytics to improve app performance and user experience
- Device information necessary for app functionality

This information is used only to provide the core functionality of the app and improve the user experience.

---

## Third-Party Services

Nutraxis may use trusted third-party services to operate certain features of the application, including:

- **Firebase (Google)** for authentication and secure data storage
- **Apple In-App Purchases** for subscription and payment processing

These services may process limited information required to provide their functionality.  
Nutraxis does not control how these third-party services handle data, and their privacy policies may apply.

---

## Data Retention

User data is stored only as long as necessary to provide the services of the application.

If a user wishes to request deletion of their data or account, they may contact the developer using the support email below.

## Health Disclaimer

Nutraxis is intended for **general wellness and informational purposes only**.

It is **not a medical application** and does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making health-related decisions.
